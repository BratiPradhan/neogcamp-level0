Exercise makes you SMARTER

THIS IS AN ERA OF A SMART JOCK.

A person who is intellectual as well as fit.

We have a misconception build up during our school time. The person who is a keetabi keeda(Indian way of saying geeky) and has no relation with exercise was consired as the most intelligent one.

I had a friend of mine, who was a footballer. He once scored more than the keetabi keeda and people were all shocked, How is that possible?

So let's see what happens.

Exercise makes our brain grow new cells and neurons. Our brain is very flexible, so with the help of exercise we can reprogram our brain in a positive manner.

  1. It increases BDNF(BRAIN DERIVED NEUROTROPHIC FACTOR). The higher the levels of BDNF are, the easier it is to learn new things.
  2. Exercise reduces stress hormones. Stress hormones prevent good sleep and memory production.
  3. Exercise produce better sleep. Better sleep means moving your studies from short term to long term.

Keep learning and exercising. You will be the SMART JOCK
