FONTS: SERIF || SANS-SERIF 😕

You must know your AUDIENCE before searching for which FONT you want. By the way this section is written in serif font.

Knowing your audience:

You should be very much sure about which part of the the society you are delivering your thoughts. If you choose to deliver your thought to kids, then you must know - they like free and easy floaing read. For them sans-serif would be the best choice. Most of the webs these day are becoming free and easy floaing because user likes simplicity. SANS-SERIF fonts are more Web friendly, modern and supports all browser screens. For printing media like books, magazine tend to have serif fonts. Serif fonts are more formal and classical. And yes, this section is written in SANS-SERIF font.

What suits web?

For web we use sans-serif fonts for larger paragraphs, articles, etc and we use serif fonts for quotes, headings, smaller section, etc. Serif fonts don't look very much attractive on mobile devices.

Conclusion:

Whichever font you choose, choose wisely.
